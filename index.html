    08 · The Way We Work
    80%
    Landfill Diversion

    Donated, Not Dumped.

    Before anything goes to the landfill, we look for a second life for it. Working furniture goes to local nonprofits. Metal, electronics, and appliances get recycled or refurbished.

    Eight out of every ten items we pick up get repurposed or donated. The other two? Disposed of properly, never dumped.

    • 10 cubic yards per truck, about a single-car-garage worth per load.
    • Donation first, disposal last. We make the calls so you don't have to.
    • Recyclables sorted on-site, not piled in one bin.
    09 · Common Questions

    Quick Answers.

    • What do you take?

      Almost anything that isn't hazardous. Furniture, appliances, electronics, yard waste, construction debris, estate items, attic and basement contents. We do not take paint, motor oil, fuel, asbestos, propane tanks, or live ammunition.

    • How fast can you come?

      Same-day service is usually available if you call before noon. Otherwise next day. Call us at 203-252-0250 and we will tell you the soonest slot.

    • How do you charge?

      By truck load. Quarter, half, three-quarter, or full (each truck holds 10 cubic yards). Price also depends on location, type of material, and accessibility. We come give you a free on-site quote before any work starts. No mystery fees, no minimum.

    • Do you take a single item?

      Yes. One couch, one mattress, one fridge. We come for jobs of any size.

    • Do you plow during storms or after?

      Usually after the storm, that's most efficient and what most clients prefer. If you want us to come during the storm too, just say so when we set up your contract. Salting and shoveling included.

    • Are you insured?

      Yes. Fully insured for residential and commercial work in Connecticut.
